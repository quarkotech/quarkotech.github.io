Quantum Software Development

Core expertise in Quantum Software Development, highlighted by our active support for Qibo, a versatile open-source quantum middleware. This platform is critical for simulation, quantum control, and calibration tasks. Quantum as a Service (QaaS/Cloud)

Specialized expertise in developing and maintaining robust Quantum as a Service (QaaS) solutions. This platform is essential for organizations adopting Qibo for their quantum computing facilities, enabling efficient execution and seamless deployment of quantum algorithms via a managed cloud infrastructure.
